About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Geography and the Environment at the University of Alabama. My research focuses on understanding the complex interactions between water systems, climate change, and human activities.

My work combines traditional hydrological methods with cutting-edge technologies including machine learning (LSTM, Transformers), remote sensing, and UAS (drone) applications to address critical water resource challenges.

I study how dams, droughts, and dredging impact river systems, floodplain dynamics, and groundwater resources across the Southeastern US, West Africa, and Central Asia.
